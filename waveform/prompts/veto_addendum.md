# Veto Addendum

When a user vetoes a song with a specific reason tag, apply the following additional constraints:

| Reason Tag | What to do |
|---|---|
| too slow | Avoid songs with BPM below the block's expected energy range. Prefer tracks at least 10 BPM faster than the vetoed song. |
| wrong genre | Stay strictly within the requested genre tags for this block. Do not blend adjacent genres. |
| overplayed | Avoid chart hits from the past 5 years. Prefer deeper cuts, B-sides, or less mainstream choices within the genre. |
| not the vibe | Recalibrate toward the block's archetype description more precisely. Re-read the archetype and energy level. |
| artist already used | Do not suggest this artist again anywhere in this session. Avoid featuring them even on collaboration tracks. |

## Positive Reinforcement

When the user keeps a song, it signals:
1. The energy level is right.
2. The genre blend is working.
3. The era and production style resonates.

Use kept songs as calibration anchors — find adjacent territory, not clones. Do not re-suggest any kept song.
